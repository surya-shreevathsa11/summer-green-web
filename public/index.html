About Us

The Summer Green Experience

Summer Green Homestay is a family-run stay located in the quiet surroundings of Madikeri, Coorg. Designed for travelers who appreciate comfort and simplicity, the homestay offers a calm space to relax while exploring the natural beauty of the region.

With thoughtfully maintained rooms and warm hospitality, Summer Green Homestay provides a welcoming environment for families, couples, and groups visiting Madikeri. Its convenient location allows guests to enjoy both peaceful surroundings and easy access to the town’s popular attractions.
